Introducing Your Touchdown Buddy(s)


TE, Travis Kelce

Considered one of the greatest tight ends of all time, Kelce is a nine-time Pro Bowler and a seven-time All-Pro, with 4 first-team and 3 second-team selections. Outside of football, Kelce has appeared on reality and scripted television and in advertisements and is in a very public relationship with singer-songwriter superstar Taylor Swift stimulating viewership and revenue for his team and its league.
